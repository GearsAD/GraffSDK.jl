Brookstone Rover

Brookstone Rover Example

In the Brookstone Rover example we implement a real-world version of the Hexagonal example, and demonstrate batch-processing in Graff using a simple $100 robot. The robot is cheap, the data is messy, and we still a good solution. Concepts highlighted: 1. Integrating Graff with MIT's LCM to process an LCM log 1. Processing offline camera data 1. Visualization
